Bridge Health Partners Data Analysis Dashboard


Welcome to the Bridge Health Partners Data Dashboard, where you can analyze current and historical healthcare data for deeper insights into patient outcomes and resource allocation. This dashboard allows you to interact with real-time data and explore historical records to support informed decision-making.

Current Analysis: you can upload and analyze your latest datasets. The results will be processed and displayed through visualizations to help you quickly understand key metrics and trends based on the input data.

Historical: you will find a list of previously analyzed datasets. Here, you can access and review the outcomes of past analyses, compare them against current data, and identify patterns over time.
